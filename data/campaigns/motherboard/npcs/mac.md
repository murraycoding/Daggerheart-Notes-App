## Features

### Commanding Presence (Passive)
Allied minions within Close range add +1 to their attack rolls.

### Call the Guard (Action)
Spend 2 Fear. Aldric calls for reinforcements — introduce a group of City Watch minions at a nearby entrance.

### Deflect Blame (Reaction)
When Aldric would take Severe damage, he shoves an adjacent minion into the attack instead. The minion takes the damage. Can only trigger once per scene.

## Notes

Aldric has been skimming city tax revenue for three years. He knows the PCs are getting close and is actively trying to frame them for the missing funds.

He keeps a ledger hidden behind a false panel in his office — the real one, not the decoy he'll hand over willingly.
